Self journeys preview

The self journeys preview page has the following layout:

  1. Button for opening the hamburger menu
  2. Button for all journeys
  3. Button for navigating to the login page
  4. Button for reserved journeys tab
  5. Button for bought journeys tab
  6. Card with basic information of one journey
  7. Button for detailed preview of the selected journey
  8. Button for previewing the next page

Here, you have two tabs for your reserved and bought journeys. By clicking on any tab you get max 4 cards that representing your journeys. Each card presents one journey and contains basic information such as the journey's name, departure and arrival dates, and price. To view more detailed information and edit a reservation or purchase of journey, you click the button labeled "View". If there are more than 4 journeys available, you will also see a "Next" button on the screen, which fetches the next 4 journeys.

On the side, there is a menu that allows you to switch to all journeys or log out of the system.

Shortcut List

  • Preview all journeys: CTRL + 1
  • Log out: CTRL + 4

All journeys preview

This is the first page that the client sees after logging in. The journeys preview page has the following layout:

  1. Button for opening the hamburger menu
  2. Button for self journeys
  3. Button for navigating to the login page
  4. Card with basic information of one journey
  5. Button for detailed preview of the selected journey
  6. Button for previewing the next page

When you log in to the system, you arrive at this screen. Here, the first 4 most recent journeys in our assortment are listed. Each card presents one journey and contains basic information such as the journey's name, departure and arrival dates, and price. To view more detailed information and make a reservation or purchase the journey, you click the button labeled "View". If there are more than 4 journeys available, you will also see a "Next" button on the screen, which fetches the next 4 journeys.

On the side, there is a menu that allows you to switch to your journeys or log out of the system.

Shortcut List

  • Preview self journeys: CTRL + 2
  • Log out: CTRL + 4
